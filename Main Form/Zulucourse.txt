Uma usondeza ku-1000th oseduzane hlola njalo inombolo ngaphambi kwenombolo yokugcina (Uma inombolo ingu-1567 bese ubheka inombolo 5 - inombolo ngaphambi kowe-1000). Ngemuva kwalokho sebenzisa isheke ukubona ukuthi kufanele lizungeze noma lehle. Uma inombolo ilingana no-5 noma inkulu kuno-5 wokuzungeza phezulu, uma inombolo ingaphansi kuka-5 we-round down. Khumbula, lapho siqoqa sandisa inkulungwane eyodwa bese sishintsha zonke ezinye izinombolo sabeka u-0 (Isb. 1567 sizosondelana ngoba inombolo engaphambi kwenkulungwane ingu-5. Ngakho-ke sizokwengeza eyodwa ngoyedwa 1 + 1 = 2 bese sibuyisela konke ezinye izinombolo ezine-0: 567 -> 000. Ngakho-ke, inombolo eyindilinga engu-1567 ingu-2000!) Lapho siqoqa inani eligciniwe sigcina inombolo eyinkulungwane ngokufanayo bese sifaka zonke ezinye izinombolo ngo-0. (Isib. 1273 zizoncipha ngoba u-2 ngaphansi kuka-5, ngakho-ke sizogcina u-1 bese simiselela u-273 sifake u-000. Ngakho-ke, inombolo eyindilinga izoba ngu-1000). #
Lapho ungeza izinkulungwane, sebenza kusuka kwesobunxele uye kwesokudla. Okokuqala engeza amayunithi, uma amayunithi engezwe ndawonye amakhulu kuno-10 bese ufaka u-1 emashumini. Ngemuva kwalokho hamba engeza amashumi, uma isamba sazo sikhulu kune-100 engeza u-1 kumakhulu nokunye. Bheka isibonelo esingezansi: #
Lapho ukhipha izinombolo, kwesokunxele kwesokudla. Khipha njalo inombolo encane kunazo zonke enombolweni enkulu (inombolo enkulu ngenombolo encane). Uma inombolo ephezulu, incane kunenombolo engezansi ithwala inombolo kusuka enombolweni ukusuka kwesobunxele. Ngakho-ke, uma unenombolo engu-473 - 129, 3 ingaphansi kuka-4 ngakho-ke siboleka ishumi kusuka ku-7 bese sishintsha lesi-3 sibe ishumi nantathu bese kuthi esingu-7 siye ku-6. yenza lokhu size sithole inombolo yethu!#
Lapho iphindaphindeka izinombolo, empeleni kuyinto efanayo nokwengeza inombolo efanayo izikhathi ze-mulitple! Ngakho-ke, uma une-9 x 4 kuyinto efana ncamashi nokusho u-9 + 9 + 9 + 9 (9 + 9 kane) okulingana no-36. Ngakho-ke, 9 x 4 = 36! #
Lapho uhlukanisa izinombolo, beka inombolo enkulu ngaphezu kwenombolo encane. Bese ubona ukuthi inombolo encane ingangena kangaki kunombolo enkulu. Isibonelo, 28/7, sibheka ukuthi kangaki u-7 angangena ku-28n. Ngakho-ke, sithi u-7 + 7 = 14 asikabi ku-24 ngakho-ke siyaqhubeka u-14 + 7 = 21 siphinde futhi u-21 + 7 = 28! Ngakho-ke, u-7 ungene kumahlandla angama-28 ama-4 -> 24/7 = 4!#
Lapho ukwenziwa lula kwamaqhezu kubheka inombolo encane kunazo zonke bese uthola zonke izici zawo (izinombolo ezingahlukanisa kuzo). Isb. 6/12 - sibheka u-6 bese sibhala phansi izici zawo: [1,2,3,6] lapho-ke uma usuka entweni ephezulu uye kwisici esiphansi bese ubona ukuthi ngabe isici siyinombolo yenombolo enkulu yini. Ngakho-ke, kulokhu into ephezulu kakhulu engu-6 ingu-6, sisebenzisa lokhu ukuhlukanisa kube yinombolo enkulu bese sibona ukuthi ngabe isici esingu-12. 12 sinezici [1,2,3,4,6,12] ngakho-ke isici! Sibe sesihlukanisa zombili izinombolo ngesithupha ukuthola impendulo yethu. 6/12 = 1/2!#
Lapho usebenzisa izilinganiso hlala uqinisekisa ukuthi izilinganiso zikwelinye unit (zonke zikumamitha noma amasentimitha). Ngemuva kwalokho konke esikwenzayo ukufaka izilinganiso ndawonye ukuthola impendulo yethu! Isb. uBob unocingo oluvaliwe lobude obungu-51m, 100cm, 90m, 1km. Lungakanani ubude bocingo? Okokuqala guqula konke kube ngamamitha (100cm -> 1m; 1km -> 1000m) bese ufaka u-1 + 1000 + 51 + 90 = 1142m #
Lapho usebenza nesikhathi konke esikwenzayo kungeza njengoba besingakwenza nge-equation ejwayelekile. Kepha, ngalokhu kunalokho lapho imizuzu ifinyelela kuma-60 sandisa amahora bese sisetha kabusha imizuzu. Isb. Imizuzu engu-12: 22 + 45 sifaka ama-45 kuye kwangama-22 = 67amaminithi -> 12:67 kepha imizuzu ingama-67 ngakho-ke esikhundleni salokho sifaka u-1 emahoreni bese ususa ama-60 kusukela kumizuzu -> 12 + 1 = 13 no-67-60 = 7 ngakho-ke ukusithola 13: 07! #
Lapho sisondela ku-equation yegama kufanele siqale sifunde umbuzo futhi siqaphele ukuthi yini inkinga (ngabe ungeza izinombolo, uphinda izinombolo, ukhipha izinombolo? Ngemuva kwalokho hlela ukuthi uzoyiphendula kanjani inkinga (engeza izinombolo noma ususe, njll), yenza uhlelo bese kukhona impendulo yakho!#
